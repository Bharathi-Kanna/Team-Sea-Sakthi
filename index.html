Team Sea Sakthi

Team Sea Sakthi is the premier boat racing team of KCT. We are the first team to represent India at the prestigious Monaco Energy Boat Challenge. We, currently in “Energy Class”, design and fabricate a capable powertrain and a cockpit to house it. Made of 15 members from varied disciplines of KCT we come together putting our minds, skill and efforts towards the challenge. With an eye towards sustainable future, we Team Sea Sakthi the team from a nation with an ocean named after her are all set to conquer the waves.
