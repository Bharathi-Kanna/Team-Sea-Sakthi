TECHNOLOGY PARTNER

NavAlt is the award-winning manufacturer of efficient and sustainable marine transportation solutions. They’ve initiated groundbreaking achievements and industry leading feats, such as India's first solar powered ferry, named ADITYA. Innovation in Solar-powered electric vessels are the core focus of NavAlt. Team Sea Sakthi has developed an enduring partnership with NavAlt, through which the student team receives technical expertise and technological aid. The propulsion and power systems of the team’s boat has been developed by NavAlt.
